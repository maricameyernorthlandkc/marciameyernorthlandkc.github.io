About
Dr. Meyer

As an experienced cognitive behaviorist, Dr. Meyer is committed to providing high quality mental health care for children, adolescents, adults, and couples in the Kansas City area. A graduate of the counseling/psychology doctoral program from the University of Missouri Kansas City, Dr. Meyer also served as an adjunct professor of the program for 15 years where she spent her time training others entering the field of therapy. She is dedicated to emphasizing trust, respect, confidentiality, and compassion. She will collaborate with her client's psychiatrist or primary care physician upon request.

Dr. Meyer is a provider for most major insurance companies and Telehealth clients are also welcome.

Dr. Meyer is currently accepting new patients. If you are interested in setting up a session, please give her a call at (816)453-2600 or submit a message in the contact form below.
