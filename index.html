About Dr. Meyer

As an experienced cognitive behaviorist, Dr. Meyer is committed to providing high quality mental health care for children, adolescents, adults, and couples counseling. A graduate of the counseling/psychology doctoral program from the University of Missouri Kansas City, Dr. Meyer also served as an adjunct professor of said program for 15 years, training others entering the field of therapy. She is dedicated to emphasizing trust, respect, confidentiality, and compassion in her therapy. She will focus on collaborating with her client's psychiatrist or primary care physician if requested. Dr. Meyer is a provider for most of the major insurance companies. Telehealth clients are welcomed.
